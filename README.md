# MANIFESTO

A premium, minimalist personal manifesto and "pocket arsenal" designed for high-performance builders and thinkers. This is not just a page of words; it is a designed psychological environment for both your best days and your hardest hours.

## 🌟 Key Features

### 1. Dual-Mode Ergonomics
- **Full Manifesto**: The complete narrative journey, structured around identity, abundance, and action.
- **Essentials Mode**: A one-tap toggle in the navigation that hides everything except the **Master Phrase**, your **Core Affirmations**, and the **Emergency Protocol**. Clean, zero-distraction focus when you have low mental bandwidth.

### 2. The Intention-Driven Arsenal (FAB)
The "Give Me A Phrase" floating action button (bottom-right) doesn't just give you random quotes. It asks: **"What do you need?"**
- **Strength**: For pushing through resistance.
- **Clarity**: For cutting through noise.
- **Perspective**: For remembering the long game.
- **Hard Days**: For survival and steady recovery.

### 3. Emergency Protocol (Break Glass)
A dedicated `#emergency-section` at the bottom of the page, accessible via the "Hard Day" quick-links in the navigation and mobile action bar. It features calm, grounding lines for when you are at your lowest point.

### 4. Interactive Daily Anchor
A dynamic daily word with built-in utility:
- **Shuffle (⟳)**: Manually rotate your daily anchor.
- **Pin (📌)**: Commit to one word—it stays pinned even if you refresh the page.
- **Copy (📋)**: Quickly copy anchors to your notes or phone wallpaper.

### 5. Mobile-Optimized Design
- **Sticky Bottom bar**: On mobile, the FAB is replaced by a native-app-style control bar for one-thumb navigation.
- **Ambient Visuals**: Dark/Light mode support with dynamic "Hero Glow" backgrounds and grain overlays for a premium, tactile feel.

## 🛠 Tech Stack
- **Pure Static HTML/CSS/JS**: Zero dependencies. Deploys anywhere as a single file.
- **Modern Layouts**: Flexbox, CSS Grid, and responsive clamp-based typography.
- **Interactions**: Intersection Observers for fade-in reveals and custom typewriter logic for the Master Phrase.

## 🚀 Getting Started
Simply open `index.html` in any modern browser.
